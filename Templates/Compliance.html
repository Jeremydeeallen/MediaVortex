Compliance

Each vertical answers one question about every MediaFile. Audio = "is audio compliant?", Video = "is video compliant?", Container = "is container compliant?". The bucket trigger composes the three answers into the final WorkBucket. Edits here take effect on the next per-vertical recompute (no service restart).

Audio Compliance Rules

System-wide policy (singleton row). Applies to every MediaFile regardless of profile. Per-scope overrides for individual libraries / folders / files live at /AudioNormalization.

Target integrated loudness (LUFS)
Where the audio cluster aims. Streaming-TV standard ~-23 to -24. YouTube/Spotify ~-14.
Target true peak (dBTP)
Ceiling for true peaks. -2 dBTP is conservative; -1 dBTP is broadcast-standard tighter.
Acceptable audio codecs (CSV)
Source codecs allowed. Anything else triggers transcode for codec.
Video Compliance Rules
Acceptable video codecs (CSV)
Files with codec NOT in this list -> needs transcode. Efficient-source check compares SourceKbps to the assigned profile's TargetKbps at the file's resolution -- edit that in the Profiles admin, not here.
Container Compliance Rules
Acceptable containers (CSV)
Files whose container is NOT in this list -> needs remux
Acceptable audio codecs (CSV)
Files whose audio codec is NOT in this list -> needs remux to re-encode audio into container
