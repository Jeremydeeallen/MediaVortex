Compliance

Each vertical answers one question about every MediaFile. Audio = "is audio compliant?", Video = "is video compliant?", Container = "is container compliant?". The bucket trigger composes the three answers into the final WorkBucket. Edits here take effect on the next per-vertical recompute (no service restart).

Audio compliance policy is managed at the full Audio Normalization settings page.

Open /AudioNormalization

Scope-cascade policy, dialog-boost tracks, language detection, operator review queue all live on the dedicated page. The audio vertical writes `AudioCompliant` + `AudioCompliantReason` on every MediaFile post-policy-evaluation.
